Why these 4 practice modes?
👁 See Arabic → Recall English
Classic active recall — the single most effective memory technique. Forcing retrieval strengthens the neural pathway.
🔄 See English → Recall Arabic
Reverse retrieval — trains production, not just recognition. Critical for speaking and writing.
👂 Listen → Identify
Auditory encoding — creates a second memory trace independent of the written form. Essential for listening comprehension.
✏️ Fill the Gap
Contextual recall — seeing a word used in a real sentence activates elaborative encoding, the deepest form of memory.
